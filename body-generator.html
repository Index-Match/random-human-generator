For generating random human faces for characters

Options

Blend Complex*
Blend Simple*

The blended options will give you an androgenous figure. Blend Simple just goes halfway between the male and female minimum, maximum and average measurements, so gives a smoother result. Blend complex actually recalculates the data for all participants, therefore allowing for greater extremes and some more unique figures.
